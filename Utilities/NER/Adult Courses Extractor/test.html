Remitted Fee

You are entitled to a reduction in your course fee if you receive any of the following benefits:

  • Jobseekers Allowance (contribution and income based)
  • Employment Support Allowance (ESA)
  • Working Tax Credit
  • Income Support
  • Council Tax/Housing Benefit
  • Guaranteed Pension Credit
  • Incapacity Benefit (including severe disability allowance)
  • Disability Living Allowance (DLA)

To claim a remitted fee you must provide evidence that you are in receipt of the benefit. This is limited to one course per term. Subsequent enrolments within one term will need to be paid at full fee.
